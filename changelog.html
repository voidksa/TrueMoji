v2.0.1

What's new

TrueMoji 2.0.1 adds NotebookLM compatibility on top of the TrueMoji 2.0 redesign, emoji picker, new packs, backup, and faster engine.

  • 📓

    NotebookLM compatibility

    TrueMoji now detects emojis inside NotebookLM quizzes, flashcards, and other delayed Shadow DOM surfaces.

  • 🎨

    Brand-new design

    Warm, sunny look that matches the TrueMoji logo crafted for both light and dark themes.

  • 📦

    Three new emoji packs

    Twemoji 15, Google Noto Color, and Microsoft Fluent Flat alongside the existing Apple, Google, Fluent 3D, Twitter, Facebook, and OpenMoji.

  • 🔍

    Built-in emoji picker

    Search, browse, and copy any emoji in your selected pack's style. Recents and favorites are saved automatically.

  • 💾

    Settings backup & restore

    Export all your TrueMoji preferences to a JSON file and import them back on another device.

  • ⚡

    Faster, more reliable engine

    Pinned CDN versions, smarter pack fallback, and per-set availability checks mean fewer broken images.
